GENERAL INSTRUCTIONS FOR ADMISSION


  • For admission at campus(offline), please visit our office during school hours.
  • For Online admission, plase follow below instructions:
    • Step 1: Please find the 'General Info' tab and click on it. You will come to a page where you can explore all of the details related to admission.
    • Step 2: When you are satisfied, click on the 'Enquiry' link on the top right corner.
    • Step 3: Fill the details.
    • Step 4: Make payment and take a screenshot of the payment confirmation page(You may be asked to produce this screenshot if need be).
