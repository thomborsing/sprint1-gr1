General Instructions for Admission
Parents/Guardians should upload only the Birth Certificate issued by Municipal Corporation or the DOB shown in the Passport. Please do not upload Birth Certificates issued by the Nursing Home/Hospital
Please upload the latest Passport sized coloured photographs of the student, parents or guardian (if applicable) on the designated place of the Application Form.
Take the printouts of the submitted Application Form and Admit Card on clean white A4 sheet paper.
Please paste passport-sized coloured photographs (must be identical to the uploaded pictures) of the student, parents or guardian on the designated place of the printed Application Form..
The date of assessment /observation/interaction/interview will be mentioned in the Admit Card and to be mailed shortly after submitting the Application Form.
